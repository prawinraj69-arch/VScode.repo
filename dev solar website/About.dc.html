About Dev Solar

A manufacturing company concentrated on solar power generating systems — established in 2011 in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu.

Company Profile

A vertically integrated solar manufacturer

In solar, we haven't stopped at merely selling products. We are one of the leading solar system integrators, providing complete solar power solutions to everyone facing acute grid-power shortage across the country.

Our solar module manufacturing unit has the plant capacity to produce more than 10 MW of solar modules per year. We are enlisted by TEDA and MNRE for solar system integration.

To overcome the widening gap between demand and supply of electrical energy, the right alternative is renewable energy — and we've built a state-of-the-art factory that produces the heart of every system: the photovoltaic module.

Our Heritage

From wet grinders to megawatts

Dev Solar is a unit of Dev International — a Coimbatore manufacturer with over 15 years in home appliances. We invented the patented armless table-top wet grinder, sold under the PVG (Price · Value · Guarantee) brand.

We supplied 5 lakh grinders to the Tamil Nadu Government welfare scheme, installed 6 wind mills across the state, and in 2011 diversified into manufacturing solar power generating systems.
